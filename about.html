Foodpath - mobile app

Foodpath Is your path to food.

Foodpath finds the food locations near you and shows you the path

Step 1 - Activate your GPS location & click the "localize me" button

Step 2 - Search address is incorrect? you can type in the correct one

Step 3 - Pick the food you're craving & get the closest locations

You can filter the choice on the basis of food, budget, diet restrictions & distance

Step 4 Click the "route" button & the map shows the way to the chosen location
